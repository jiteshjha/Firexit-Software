WHAT IS FIREXIT

Firexit Software Pvt. Ltd. is a video game development company based in Manipal, India. Founded in early 2014, and incubated at Manipal University Technology Business Incubator.

We develop and independently publish Android titles that have been played by millions of users. With expertise in Unity3D, we also provide game development services.

Our R&D work includes computer vision applications and stereoscopic 3D systems.

Watch out for Firexit's upcoming game Speed Street 2, and our amazing user acquisition and engagement tools.

WHO IS FIREXIT

VATSAL AMBASTHA

CEO and Team Lead

Vatsal has been playing video games since he was four, the first one he remembers playing was Jordan Mechner’s Prince Of Persia on a grayscale screen.

He started game development at 15, and released his first Android game "XPro Rally" at 17. He did freelance work for a bit, then founded Firexit Software in early 2014, taking the entrepreneurial road.

A huge fan of Spoon and Nada Surf and good at playing only FPS games, he spends most of his day in the office, working on awesome stuff with the Firexit team and handling the business.

ISHAAN KHANNA

Unity3D Programmer

Ishaan is a Computer Science techie in-the-making and at MIT, Manipal. Having been an avid gamer he started game development at 19. He presently codes games for Firexit, an entrepreneurial venture with his friend and college classmate, Vatsal.

His hobbies include travelling, playing with his Kinect and telling everyone about how his college is adjacent to a beach.

SHUBHAM VERMA

Researcher

Shubham is the Researcher in R&D unit at Firexit Software. He is currently pursuing his Bachelor’s Degree in CSE at MIT, Manipal where he is also a member at Computer Vision Research Group (CVRG).

His research areas include Computer Vision, AR and Medical Computer Vision with particular interest in Image Processing, Machine Learning, Gesture/Pose recognition, Stereo Vision, 3D reconstruction and Retinal Image Analysis.

His brother Er. Naimish Verma is a source of inspiration for him. He is interested in healthcare and public welfare projects too. As a member as CVRG he actively contributed his highly efficient code for the projects aimed at welfare of physically-challenged people.

He is also the AI Systems Head at Project Manas, an initiative to make a self driving car.
