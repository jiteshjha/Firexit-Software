Services & Tech

Firexit Software Pvt. Ltd. is a video game development company based in Manipal, India. Founded in early 2014, and incubated at Manipal University Technology Business Incubator.

We develop and independently publish Android titles that have been played by millions of users. With expertise in Unity3D, we also provide game development services.

Our R&D work includes computer vision applications and stereoscopic 3D systems.

Watch out for Firexit's upcoming game Speed Street 2, and our amazing user acquisition and engagement tools.
